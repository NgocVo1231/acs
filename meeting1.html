What did we talk about in relations to Asian Cultures?

We talked about Mid-autumn Festival. It's a popular holiday in East and Southeast Asia where children meet up with their lanterns, hang out and to honor the moon.

Next, we showed general members the Asian Squat. The squat comes in handy when it comes to commercing in outdoor markets. Many have to work from early morning to late afternoon. This stance allow the people to squat for longer.

Ho Chi Minh City (Saigon)

In my opinion, the best city in Vietnam. Think of Saigon as the Los Angeles or New York City of Vietnam. This is where most Vietnamese around the country gather to start their careers. You might run into celebrities and catch them eating Pho at a local restaurant. The people are humble and genuine. The traffic might be hard to get around. If you can drive in Saigon, you can drive ANYWHERE. If you ever visit Vietnam, Saigon should be on the top of your list.

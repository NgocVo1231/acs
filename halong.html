Halong Bay

A famous UNESCO World Heritage Site, Ha Long Bay contains thousands of limestone islands that are topped with rainforests. The view is manificent, in addition to its blue and clear water. Tourists often travel on boats or cruises to different locations on the bay. Many people all around the world come to visit every year.

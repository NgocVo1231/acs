My Tho

My Tho is located to the south of Ho Chi Minh City. Very famous for their coconut products like candy, snacks, and any type of cuisines or manufactured products that's coconut tree related.

We got the chance to experience the "Mien Tay" life. Life here is more simple and most people either farm or own a small little business. The population isn't as crowded as the city. The people are kind and friendly. Their cuisines is an 11/10.

Ancient houses in the countryside are sometimes enterly made out of wood. For example, this house was made out of only coconut trees.

One of the popular music genre is "Cai Luong". I will be honest. It is not pop. You will rarely find teenagers sing "Cai Luong". However, the genre is incredibly unique and special among the Vietnamese people.

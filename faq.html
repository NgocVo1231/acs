What is the Asian Cultural Society?

We are a club at Marshfield High School. Our program focuses on researching, learning and sharing about Asian Cultures. It's a safe space for the curious minds.

Could only Asian students join the Asian Cultural Society?

No, culture is to share and to learn from each other. We encourage all students to join!

When do the meetings take place?

They are usually the first two fridays of the month and the last two mondays of the month due to our advisor's meeting schedule.

What are the positions for the committee?

The committee consists of 1 president, 1 vice president, 1 historian, 1 treasurer, 1 technician, 1 secretary and 1 publicity coordinator.

Are there any prerequesites to run for a position in the committee?

The only prereq is probably the ability to fully commit! President and Vice President should have been in the club for at least 1 or 2 years and technicians should've at least taken a computer science course.

Could I still join if I missed the first meeting or it's the middle of the year?

Yes you can. Feel free to stop by whenever you are available. We respect your priorities and welcomes everyone.
